About Jay Chou

Brief introduction

Taiwan Chinese pop singer, famous musician, music creator, composer, lyricist, producer, one of the owners and director of Javel music company. In recent years, he has been involved in the film industry. Jay Chou is the most revolutionary and indicative singer songwriter in Asian pop music after 2000, known as the "king of Asian pop". He broke through the original theme and form of Asian music, integrated multiple music materials, and created a variety of song styles, especially hip-hop or rhythm blues, which integrated Chinese and Western styles. It can be said that he is the pioneer of creating "Chinese style" of Chinese pop music. The appearance of Jay Chou has broken the stagnant situation of Asian pop music and opened a new page for Asian pop music!
